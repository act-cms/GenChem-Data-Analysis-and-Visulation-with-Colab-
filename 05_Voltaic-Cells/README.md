Lesson 05: Impact of Concentrations on Voltaic Cell Potentials
=======================

The purpose of this lesson is for students to perform data modeling for a voltaic cell under different conditions.

## Lesson Information
### Lesson Learning Outcomes
At the end of this lesson, students will be able to...
1. Interpret how concentration changes influence cell potential.
2. Graph E_cell vs. concentrations, Q, or log⁡Q to identify trends.
3. Use E_cell vs. log⁡Q to determine n, E_cell^o, and K, and compare with theoretical predictions.

### Cyberinfrastructure Prerequisites

Students will use Google Colab and Gemini. No prior programming experience is required. 

### Content Prerequisites

Before beginning this lesson, students are expected to be familiar with the
following content topics:

* Electrochemistry: electromotive force
* Electrochemistry: Voltaic cells
* Electrochemistry: electrochemical work
